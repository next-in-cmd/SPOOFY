How Spoofy Deepfake Detector Works

Spoofy Deepfake Detection is designed to safeguard digital identity verification against fraud. It identifies whether a face in an image or video is AI-generated, manipulated, or authentic. This technology helps prevent criminals from bypassing digital identity checks by using deepfakes to impersonate others.

1

Data Collection

Generate huge ethnical and gender-diverse data sets of real and (deep) fake faces.

2

Deepfake Identification

Train AI to recognize the differences between authentic and potentially manipulated content.

3

Authentication

Integrate deepfake detection software tools into verification services for liveness detection and biometric authentication.
